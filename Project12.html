Tool Box

The Brief

This was actually a gift for my Dad. He asked for some needle files. When I got the files, I was not happy with the storage solution, so I decided to design my own casing.

Cushiony Tools

The files are placed in a custom-cut foam brick with bespoke slots for each file making sure they are comfortable in their box and well looked after.

Final Design

The lid is acrylic and slides smoothly out allowing for access to the files. The grooves for the lid were done by experimenting with the laser cutter power/ speed settings to achieve different depth cuts. The black acrylic is lightly sanded to give a matt finish, minimising risk of nasty fingerprints ruining the slick design.

Skills

I had not particularly done much CAD for laser cutting foam before, so it was a new material I was using.
