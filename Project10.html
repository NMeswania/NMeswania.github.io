Arduino Controlled Drone

The Brief

What an exciting summer project this was! I wanted to make a medium sized drone from scratch that received instructions from a remote and had some gyroscopic-balancing/ motion-smoothing using an accelerometer.

Challenges

The biggest challenge was balancing the stabilisation controls with the instructions I send via the remote.

Development

I decided LEGO Technic seemed like the cheapest and most flexible way to make the frame, plus I have plenty of LEGO Technic. Most Drones communicate through radio frequency, so naturally I did the same using the nRF24L01 radio frequency module.

Final Design

The final design is as you have seen in the pictures, with some slight updates to the software in terms of how it combines the stabilisation and remote instructions.
